About Mercor

Mercor is training models that predict how well someone will perform on a job better than a human can. Similar to how a human would review a resume, conduct an interview, and decide who to hire, we automate all of those processes with LLMs. Our technology is so effective it’s used by all of the top 5 AI labs.

We crossed a $100M revenue run rate and have averaged 59% month over month growth for the last 6 months, making us the fastest growing company in the world. The team is small and we remain extremely profitable because we can’t hire great people as fast as revenue is growing.

About the Role

As a dedicated recruiter, you will own the full‑cycle hiring process across every function—engineering, machine learning, product, go‑to‑market, and operations. You’ll partner directly with the founders and senior leaders to build the teams that fuel Mercor’s next phase of hyper‑growth.

Your north‑star metric is straightforward: hire mission‑critical talent fast—without compromising on quality. 

This role reports to the Chief Operating Officer and sits in our San Francisco HQ. Our team works six days a week.

Key Responsibilities

  • Own the full recruiting funnel: creative sourcing, screening, interviews, closing, and onboarding.

  • Leverage Mercor’s proprietary LLM tools to triage applicants and predict quality‑of‑hire; continuously feed hiring data back to the product team.

  • Partner with founders & VPs to forecast headcount, define scorecards, and execute hiring plans.

  • Design and iterate scalable processes (ATS workflows, structured interviews, decision rubrics) that maintain a low time‑to‑hire while raising our hiring bar.

  • Build a magnetic employer brand: craft job ads, social content, events, and outreach campaigns that attract top talent worldwide.

  • Track and report recruiting KPIs (time‑to‑hire, pass‑through rates, diversity metrics, new‑hire productivity) and surface insights to leadership weekly.

What We’re Looking For

  • 2–5 + years of full‑cycle recruiting experience in fast‑growing tech, venture‑backed startups, or top search firms.

  • Demonstrated success filling technical and business roles at pace.

  • Exceptional communicator & closer—you can pitch Mercor’s mission, negotiate offers, and build rapport with executives and junior candidates alike.

  • Bias for action and willingness to work 6 days per week onsite in San Francisco (we move at start‑up speed).

Compensation & Perks

  • Performance Bonus: significant cash and equity tied to hiring targets

  • Equity Grant: generous initial grant, 4‑year vest with 1‑year cliff.

  • Relocation Bonus: $20K for moves to the Bay Area

  • Housing Bonus: $10K if you live within 0.5 mi of HQ

  • Monthly Stipend: $1K for meals

  • Health: full medical, dental, and vision coverage

  • Wellness: free Equinox membership, on‑site meals & snacks

Why Mercor?

You’ll recruit elite talent to build a product that reinvents how every company in the world hires. You’ll have autonomy, resources, and bleeding‑edge AI tools at your disposal—and you’ll see your hires’ impact on revenue within days, not quarters.

We consider all qualified applicants without regard to legally protected characteristics and provide reasonable accommodations upon request.
